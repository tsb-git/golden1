House Rules

Hotel Rules & Regulations

Tariff

The tariff is for the room only and is exclusive of any government taxes applicable . Meals and other services are available at extra cost . To know your room tariff , please contact the duty manager , guest register forms must be signed on arrivals .

Settement of Bills

Bills must be settled on presentation either by cash or valid Credit/Debit cards . Personal cheque are not accepted .

Check-in

Guests must present their Voter ID Card , Passport , Aadhar Card ,or Driving License upon Check-in time . Foreign Guestes are requested to produce their Passport and Visa upon Check-in-time.

Departure

Check out time is (11:00am) please inform the reception if you wish to retain your room beyond this time . Extension will be given dependeing on the availabilty of the room . Normal tariff will be charged . On failure of the guest to vacate the room on expiry the management shall have the right to remove the guest and his/her belongings from the room occupied by the guest .

Guest’s Belongings

Guests are particularly requested to lock the door of their rooms when going out or going bed . The management will not in any way whatsoever be responsible for any loss or damage to the guest’s belongings or any other property from either the hotel room .

Consumption of intoxicant materials

Consumption of wines or any intoxicant materials are not allowed in the rooms & within Hotel Premises .

Visitors

Persons who are not checked in the hotel may stay in a hotel room as a visitor of the existing guest from 7:00 am till 10:00pm . Beyond that time the person have to check-in and will be charged

Amendent of Rules

The management reserves to itself the right to add , to alter and to amend any of the above terms , conditions and rules .

Pets

Pets are not allowed in the hotel .

Hazardous Goods

Bringing goods and/or of raw or exposed cinema films or any other article of a combustible or hazardous nature : prohibited goods and/or a goods of objectionable nature is prohibited . The guest shall be solely liable and responsible to the management , its other guests , invitees ‘ visitors , agents and sevants for all loss financial or otherwise and damage that may be caused by such articles or as a result of the guest’s own negligence and non observance of any instructions , Gambling , contraband , prostitution , weapons , explosives , flammable objects , poisons , drugs , animals and pungent food are strictly prohibited on hotel premises .

Damage to property

The guests will be held responsible for any loss or damage to the hotel property caused by themselves , their guests or any person for whom they are responsible .

Relation between management and guest

The guests are required to deposite the room card before going out or checking out from the hotel , in case of loss of card the guest will be charged Rs 500/- for the same .

Prohibited Things :

1. Guests are prohibited to wash their cloths in the hotel bathrooms

2. Use of electrical iron and heater are prohibited

3. Smoking is prohibited in common or public areas

Return time

The hotel gate at 12AM , so the guests are requested to return to the hotel before 12AM.

Government rules and regulations and application of laws

Guest are requested to observe, abide by , confirm to and be bound by all applicable acts and laws and government rules and regulations in force from time to time .
